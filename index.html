🎯 The problem

Why does Yachay Lab exist?

In Peru and Latin America, more than 70% of rural schools have no science lab. Students memorize formulas without understanding the real phenomenon behind them.

Yachay Lab turns any browser-enabled device into a complete lab — with real-time animated physics, interactive chemistry and an AI tutor that guides without giving direct answers.
